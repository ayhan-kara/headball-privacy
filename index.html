Head Ball MR : Football Challenge

A fast-paced, real-time multiplayer MR soccer experience by Dark VR

About

Head Ball VR puts you in the heart of the action with 2v2, 3v3, or epic 5v5 matches. Dive into fully immersive virtual stadiums, customize your avatar and kits, and compete against players worldwide.

  • Multiplayer Matches: Join quick matchmaking for 2v2, 3v3, or 5v5 battles. Team up with friends or challenge opponents around the globe.
  • Dynamic Camera Angles: Switch between first-person goalkeeper view, sideline perspectives, and overhead angles to strategize every play.
  • Customizable Avatars & Kits: Unlock new jerseys, boots, and accessories as you climb the leaderboards and personalize your character.
  • Progression & Rewards: Earn experience points and in-game currency with each victory. Level up to unlock exclusive stadiums, soccer balls, and cosmetic items.
  • Realistic Ball Physics: Experience authentic ball movement and environmental effects—wind, turf friction, and player collisions—powered by a next-generation VR physics engine.
  • Voice & Gesture Controls: Coordinate with teammates using built-in voice chat and perform precise dribbles, passes, and shots with intuitive hand tracking.
